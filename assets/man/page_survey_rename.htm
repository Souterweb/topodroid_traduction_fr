SURVEY RENAME

This dialog is opened from the Rename menu of the Survey Info window. The menu is present only at "tester" level.

It has two actions.

  • Rename the survey
  • Add a prefix to all the survey stations.
Buttons:
  • Close closes the dialog.

Survey rename The "section" points have the survey name written in the option "-scrap". The survey name is automatically updated the next time the sketch is opened in TopoDroid.
